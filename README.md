# Echo

**Echo** is a private, minimalist journaling app that bridges the gap between music and emotional reflection. Capture your memories, tag your moods, and anchor them to the music you were listening to in that moment.

## Quick Start

* **Music-First:** Every entry is linked to verified track metadata via the Spotify API.
* **Private:** No social feeds, no sharing. Your archive is for your eyes only.
* **Quick Capture:** Designed to record reflections in under 20 seconds.
* **Responsive PWA:** Works beautifully on both desktop and mobile browsers.

## Technical Foundation

* **Stack:** Next.js, Supabase (PostgreSQL), and Prisma.
* **Design:** Strict 4px spacing, token-based styling, and a clean, minimalist aesthetic.
* **Security:** Server-side Zod validation, row-level tenant scoping, and encrypted sessions.

## Built for Reflection

Echo focuses on the "Music-First" connection. Whether you're feeling nostalgic, energetic, or calm, Echo turns your listening habits into a structured, private emotional archive.
